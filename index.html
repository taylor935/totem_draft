Our Māhi

At the University of Canterbury Māori and Pasifika Engineering Students’ Society, we strive to foster a thriving community by weaving together cultural identity and academic excellence. We are dedicated to supporting our members through their engineering journey—connecting students with industry mentors, providing academic tautoko, and cultivating an inclusive environment where Whanaungatanga and Pasifika values are at the heart of everything we do. Our mission is to empower the next generation of Māori and Pasifika engineers to lead both professionally and within their communities, building lasting networks and lifelong friendships. Our Partners

The Māori and Pasifika Engineering Students’ Society is a non-profit organisation dedicated to creating an engaging, professional, and culturally grounded environment for our members. The generous support from our industry partners is vital, as it enables us to provide the resources, workshops, and networking opportunities that empower our students to thrive. We sincerely thank our partners for their commitment to fostering diversity within the engineering profession and for walking alongside us in our mission.
